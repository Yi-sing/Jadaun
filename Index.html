About Jadaun Law College

Pioneering Legal Education Since 1995

Jadaun Law College was established with a vision to create competent legal professionals who can contribute to the justice system and society. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most reputed law colleges in the region.

Our mission is to provide quality legal education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for the challenges of the legal profession.

We are recognized by the Bar Council of India and affiliated with the State University, ensuring that our degrees are valued nationwide.

Detailed Eligibility Criteria

Academic Qualifications:

Candidate must have completed a Bachelor's degree (3 or 4 years) from a recognized university with minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).

Appearing Candidates:

Students in their final year of graduation may apply provisionally, but must submit final mark sheet by July 31, 2023.

Entrance Exam Exemptions:

Candidates with valid CLAT (60+ percentile) or LSAT (150+ score) may be exempted from JLCE.

Reservation Policy:

  • SC: 15%
  • ST: 7.5%
  • OBC: 27%
  • EWS: 10%
  • PwD: 5% (horizontal)
